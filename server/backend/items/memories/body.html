Memories is an interactive experience, written in WebGL and Javascript. As the power of Rendering engines at the time (2014) was underwhelming, memories use a combination of GPU and CPU rendering.

Shaders are used in order to render the Model, Flare, Clouds, Lighting as well as post-processing effects such as the edge blur.

Traditional JavaScript is used to animate the model, pull pictures out of facebook and render them into the canvas.
